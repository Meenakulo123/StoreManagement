    Rice

    Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza sativa or, less commonly, O. glaberrima.

  • Lentils

    Lentils are low in sodium and saturated fat, and high in potassium, fiber, folate, and plant chemicals called polyphenols that have antioxidant activity..
    Types of Lentils Avaliable:Split Yellow Moong Dal, Whole Masoor Dal, Split Orange Masoor Dal, Whole Green Moong Dal, Split Green Moong Dal, Whole Black Urad Dal, Split Black Urad Dal, Split Chana Dal, Bengal Gram Dal,ect.

  • Flour

    Flour, finely ground cereal grains or other starchy portions of plants, used in various food products and as a basic ingredient of baked goods. Flour made from wheat grains is the most satisfactory type for baked products that require spongy structure.
    Types of Flour Avaliable:Whole Wheat Flour, Besan, Rice Flour, Maida, Multigrain Atta, Ragi flour, etc
